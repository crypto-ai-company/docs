## Key Metrics, Ratios, and Indicators

Key metrics, ratios, and indicators are essential to understanding your data. They can help you evaluate the performance of your investments, assess the financial health of a cryptocurrency, and identify potential risks and opportunities. By analyzing key metrics, ratios, and indicators, you can make informed decisions and improve your investment strategies.
